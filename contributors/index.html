Contributors to the Coronavirus Kindness Project

Help Find and Share Stories of Acts of Kindness Around the World

The Coronavirus Kindness Project was launched in April 2020 to track and visualize news stories of kindness and acts of humanity as the world collectively faces the COVID-19 pandemic. We invite you to help spread stories of acts of kindness around the world. As a colaborator, your involvement and a brief tagline will be advertised on this page. We ask that our curators help identify and submit a minimum of 20 stories each week. All involvement and partnership with the Coronavirus Kindness Project is voluntary and non-compensated. Thank you for helping us show that Kindness is Contagious!
