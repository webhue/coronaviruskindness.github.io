Kindness Amid the Coronavirus

Tracking Stories of Kindness and Humanity related to the Pandemic

The Coronavirus Kindness Project was launched in April 2020 to track and visualize news stories of kindness and acts of humanity as the world collectively faces the COVID-19 pandemic. While we consume the barrage of information related to the pandemic, its predominantly negative nature may result in increased anxiety and take a toll on our mental health. However, stories of humanity and kindness shine through! The goal of this project is to cultivate and share those stories; to be a centralized location of the positivity, kindness, and humanity of our local, national, and global neighbours.
